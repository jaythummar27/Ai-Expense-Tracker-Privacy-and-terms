By downloading and using the App, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 SMS / Text Messages

The App requests the READ_SMS and RECEIVE_SMS permissions solely and exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To automatically detect bank transaction alerts from Indian financial institutions.
  • To parse debit/credit transaction amounts, merchant names, and dates from bank SMS notifications.
  • To eliminate the need for manual expense entry.

What we read: Only messages from known bank sender IDs (e.g., VM-HDFCBK, AD-SBIBNK, JD-ICICIB) that contain keywords like "debited," "credited," "Rs.," "UPI."

What we NEVER read:

  • Personal conversations with friends, family, or contacts
  • OTP (One-Time Password) messages
  • Social media notifications
  • Promotional messages
  • Any message not related to a financial transaction

2.2 Camera

The App requests CAMERA permission solely to:

  • Allow users to capture photos of physical receipts
  • Extract transaction amounts and merchant names from receipt images using Google ML Kit OCR technology
  • Auto-fill the expense entry form with extracted data

All image processing is performed entirely on-device. Captured images are never stored to disk, never uploaded to any server, and are immediately discarded after OCR processing is complete.

2.3 Internet Access

The App uses INTERNET permission for:

  • Gemini AI: Sending anonymized, aggregated spending summaries (no transaction IDs, no personal identifiers) to Google's Gemini API to generate personalized saving tips. You can use the App without enabling the AI Coach feature.
  • Google Drive Backup: Uploading an encrypted backup file of your transaction data to your personal Google Drive account (using the private DRIVE_APPDATA scope — not visible to other apps or Drive file lists). This is optional and only occurs when you explicitly tap "Backup to Drive."
  • No other data is transmitted.

2.4 Data You Provide Manually

When you manually add transactions, we store:

  • Transaction title, amount, type (income/expense), category, account name, date, and optional notes
  • Budget limits you set per category

This data is stored locally on your device in an SQLite database managed by Android Room.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All transaction and budget data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database on your device using Android Room.
  • Google Drive: If you opt-in to backup, data is stored in your personal Google Drive's private AppData folder (inaccessible to other apps or people).
  • No Our Servers: JayStudio does not operate any backend servers that store your personal or financial data.
  • Encryption: Android's built-in file system encryption protects all locally stored data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data (it's stored locally on your device)
  • Delete all data via Settings → Delete All Data
  • Export your data via Settings → Export CSV
  • Withdraw any permission at any time via Android Settings → App Permissions
  • Disable SMS tracking at any time from the Settings screen without losing manually entered data
